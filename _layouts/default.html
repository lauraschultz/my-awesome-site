We are

We are FIRST robotics team 2491 No Mythic from St. Paul, Minnesota. Made up of three small charter schools, we are the only metro Minnesota team to win a regional in the 2016 season! Come visit us at the FIRST World Championships!

Once myths were that of legend, Gods and Goddesses, Olympus and the Underworld. We used myths to explain what we couldn’t, attributing what we couldn’t comprehend to the powers of the gods. With science we can now take what used to be powers of the gods and explain them, turning fire stolen from the gods into a cloud of burning gas and vengeance from the gods into plate tectonics and changing climates. Myths told of people greater than we had ever seen; capable of amazing feats that the mind could barely grasp. But now we have to be those heroes, taking what was once considered magic or the power of god and harnessing it like Prometheus. Of course we create beautiful complex machines, but they are just a catalyst to help us to become what we are destined to be: a new generation of scientists, engineers and inventors. In a world free of myths, we are No Mythic.

Meet Helios

With a fast drive train and two seperate systems that can lift both totes and recycling containers, Helios is ready to take on any challenge that comes it's way.

Quick Stats:

Name:Helios
Weight:116 lbs
Dimensions:26.5 x 26.5 x 78 inches
Drive Train:Omni O-Drive
Tote Capabilities:Can stack four totes
Container Capabilities:Can add a recycling container to a 5 tote stack
Autonomous function: Can either pick up a container from the step or pick up a coopertition tote and container from the staging zone

What is FIRST?

FIRST is an organization with the mission of inspiring young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
